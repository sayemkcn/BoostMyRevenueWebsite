Why ShareMyRevenue

We understand, every apps are made for some specific purposes, But we are not trying to make the main purpose of a user to earn money, we are making sure that you stay one step ahead of your competetors. Thats it.

Okay, Then how'll your users make money by using your application?
- So simple. Because you'll share a percentage of your revenue to your users every month.
You can ask, well, why would in the earth we do that?
- BECAUSE YOU'LL EARN MORE IN THE FUTURE SO THAT YOU CAN SHARE MORE!! Awesome! isn't it?
Will it conflict with admob policy?
That's the tricky question. Because, NO! You will never encourage clicking ads by using our service. You'll just record their interactions by logging events like Firebase Analytics, remember? Yes, these events will be analysed to our server and automatically calculate users revenue based on your sharing amount next month. Any advertising network has nothing to do with it.
